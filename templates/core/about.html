The Project

What is SnapHire BD?

SnapHire BD is a two-sided marketplace that connects photography clients with professional photographers across Bangladesh. It replaces the informal, WhatsApp-based hiring process with a structured platform built on trust, portfolios, and verified reviews.

Built as a university project using Django, it demonstrates real-world marketplace architecture — role-based access, media uploads, booking flows, and fraud-resistant reviews.

❌ The Problem
Before SnapHire
  • Photographers found through word-of-mouth only
  • No way to compare portfolios or prices
  • Bookings done informally over WhatsApp
  • No accountability — fake reviews possible
  • No booking records or dispute resolution
✅ The Solution
With SnapHire BD
  • Browse verified photographer profiles & portfolios
  • Compare rates, location, and experience
  • Structured booking with status tracking
  • Reviews unlocked only after confirmed booking
  • Full dashboard for clients, photographers & admins
